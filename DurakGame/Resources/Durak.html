Durak Rules
  1. There are no winners only a loser, the Durak(fool)
  2. The objective of the game is to get rid of your hand so that you the player does not become the durak. The durak is the last
    player with cards left in their hands and they are the loser.
  3. Durak can be played with 3 decks:
    • A deck of 36, the cards ranking high to low: ace, king, queen, jack, 10, 9, 8, 7, 6.
    • A deck of 20, the cards ranking high to low: ace, king, queen, jack, 10.
    • And a standard deck of 52 cards ace being the highest and 2 being the lowest.
  4. Each player is given 6 cards
  5. Once all the players have cards the next card in the deck is flipped over and the suit of that card is the trump.
  6. The trump is basically the best cards in the game and can beat any other card except for another trump card that is a higher level.
  7. This game is about attacking and defending, game goes in the clockwise order and the dealer starts
  8. The attacker may be helped by other players
  9. The defender can not be help by anyone
  10. The attacker begins by playing any card from hand face up on the table in front of the defender.
  11. To beat off the attack the defender will have to beat this and all subsequent attacking cards.
  12. If the defender wins 6 battles or doesn't have any cards left, they have successfully defended the attack and the cards are put
    into the discard pile, but if the defender can not defend the attack the attack will be successful and all the cards involved in
    that battle will be picked up by the defender
    13. .
  13. Whenever an attack or defend is successful any player that does not have 6 cards must pick up cards until they have 6 cards in their
    hand in a clock wise order.
  14. If there is no cards left in the talon(deck) any players still needs a card must pick up the trump card.
  15. Once there is no cards left in the talon and a player runs out of cards they are out of the game and safe from becoming durak
  16. The game is over when there is only one player with cards left in their hands, they are the durak.
